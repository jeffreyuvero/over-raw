Welcome to Over Raw

At Over Raw, we are dedicated to empowering new developers and fostering collaboration within the tech industry. Our mission is to provide a supportive and inclusive environment where aspiring programmers can refine their skills, build impressive portfolios, and connect with mentors and peers.

Through a variety of programs, workshops, and mentorship initiatives, we aim to equip new developers with the tools, knowledge, and opportunities they need to succeed in their careers. We believe that by nurturing raw talent and fostering a culture of collaboration and innovation, we can create a stronger and more vibrant tech community that benefits individuals and organizations alike.

Join us at Over Raw as we work together to unleash the potential of new developers and build a brighter future for the tech industry. Together, we can pave the way for innovation, creativity, and collaboration in the ever-evolving world of technology.
